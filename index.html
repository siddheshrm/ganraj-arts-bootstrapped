About Us

Experience

Founded in the late 90s, we embarked on a remarkable journey to revive the art of traditional Ganesh murti making. With a deep-rooted understanding of the rich cultural significance behind these artifacts, we set out to honor our heritage while treading gently on the planet we call home. Over the years, we have honed our skills, blending age-old techniques with innovative sustainable practices to create murtis that are not only visually captivating but also ethically produced.

Traditional

At Shree Ganraj Arts Studio, we hold the traditional craftsmanship of Ganesh murtis making close to our hearts. Our team of skilled artisans, who have inherited this expertise from generations past, pour their heart and soul into each creation. By ensuring that our murtis retain an unparalleled authenticity and aesthetic appeal, embodying the spirit of the bygone era.

Eco-friendly

We believe that reverence for tradition should go hand in hand with a deep respect for nature. Embracing this ideology, we have made a conscious choice to prioritize eco-friendly practices throughout our production process.

Customisation

Understanding the personal significance and emotions behind Ganesh murtis or any other art, we offer custom creations that cater to individual preferences and requirements. Our artisans imbue each creation with the love and care it deserves.
